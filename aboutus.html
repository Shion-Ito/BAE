About Us

Here at Texas A&M, we are proud to present one of the newest student-led organizations that connects students across all engineering fields. Brothers in Aggie Engineering aims to give our members convenient access to resources they need for personal success, which includes but are not limited to mentorship, community service, tutorials, and academic development. In keeping with the Aggie family core, we strive to forge the best version of our engineering program with a bright future ahead for all of our members.

“There is nothing I believe more strongly than getting young people interested in science and engineering, for a better tomorrow, for all humankind.” – Bill Nye


Recent Activities
